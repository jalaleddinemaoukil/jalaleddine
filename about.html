ABOUT ME

Salam! I’m Jalal Eddine Maoukil, a Web Developer & Designer from Morocco and an entrepreneur navigating the crossroads of art, history, technology, and beyond. I’m passionate about turning ideas into beautiful websites and AI automations that make life easier.

Salam! I’m Jalal Eddine Maoukil, a Web Developer & Designer from Morocco and an entrepreneur navigating the crossroads of art, history, technology, and beyond. I’m passionate about turning ideas into beautiful websites and AI automations that make life easier.

My true passion is creating websites and designing templates in Framer, a platform that lets me blend art with functionality. Every morning, I start my day with a cup of Moroccan Tea and a mind full of new ideas, ready to bring them to life on screen.
